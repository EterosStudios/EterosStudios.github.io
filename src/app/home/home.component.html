About Us

ETEROS Studios was founded in 2020 in Thessaloniki, Greece.

The studio has a flat hierarchy and all members are equals.

We work through co-operation and not coercion. Our labor is created with care and respect, and as such all games created are fully representative of us.

We think of video games as art, not as products.
